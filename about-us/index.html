    I start my day being awaken by the alarm clock, I tend to set a few of those because sometimes I turn them off while I'm still asleep. Then I go to the br to get ready for the day; after leaving the br I get myself dressed and head to the kitchen to make some breakfast and prepare my lunch. When I'm ready for the day, I go to my pc and start checking the stuff I'm doing and advance a bit more on it. When it's around 12:40 on the clock I put everything in my backpack and leave the house to come to Riwi, here I learn how to program, but they also teach me other useful abilities for my life and english. At 10pm I leave Riwi and head to my home, it takes me around an hour to get there and when I finally arrive I search for something to eat, rest a little bit and then I begin checking the stuff I learned during the day, and I try to advance as much as I can before sleepiness gets the best of me and makes me fall asleep.
